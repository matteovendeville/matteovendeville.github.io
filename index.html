Chasing Light

I am Mattéo Vendeville, a landscape photographer based in Grenoble, France, gateway to the Alps and the wild. My work is driven by a single obsession: light. The fleeting, untameable, transformative light that turns an ordinary landscape into something beyond words.

I photograph mountains, forests, coastlines and night skies : wherever nature is raw and uncompromised by human presence. Each image is the result of hours of waiting, walking and watching the world breathe. I start photography when I was 14. My biggest inspiration in landscape photography is Marc Adamus. I use a canon eos 7d with a canon 10-22mm f/3.5-5.6.

"Photography is about patience"
